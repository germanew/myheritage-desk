10. Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q1: How long does it take to get DNA results?

    • Typically 3–6 weeks once the lab receives your kit. Shipping delays vary based on location. Contact support for tracking or delays beyond 8 weeks.

  • Q2: Can I pause my subscription?

    • There is no “pause.” You can downgrade to free or lower-tier plans at the end of the current cycle. You’ll retain access through the paid term.

  • Q3: What if I lose my DNA activation code?

    • Log in to your account, navigate to “DNA kits,” and reprint the code or order a replacement. Customer service can also assist in retrieving the code.

  • Q4: How do I share my family tree with relatives?

    • In “Family Tree Settings,” generate a private invitation link or adjust privacy levels. Support can guide sharing options based on your subscription type.

  • Q5: Can I cancel internationally and get a refund?

    • Yes, within the refund window (usually 14 days for plans, 30 days for kits). International purchasers should request cancellation via the support portal or call the USA helpline.
